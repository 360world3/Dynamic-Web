Help
Some tips to get you started:

Your location is marked with the coffee cup icon, tapping this icon will show the level of accuracy to which your location is being displayed.

Coffee bean markers are coffee shops from our database, tapping these icons will give you additional information about the shop:
- Tapping the name will take you to their website
- Tap the phone number to place a call to that shop
- Press get directions to have display a route you can follow by road to navigate to the shop from your location.

Although you can use native zoom functionality on devices such as pinch to zoom addition zoom controls are available on the top left of the screen.

The three line icon on the menu on the top right represents the list view function which allows you to view your closest coffee shops in a list instead of a man.

The person icon on the top right menu allows you to sign up for our mailing list and is also where functionality for our Loyalty scheme will be added in the future.

witching to normal map view allows you to change the style of the map to a more detailed conventional view of the map if you have difficulty using the default style.
